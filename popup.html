Before you start: Make sure you are logged in to your account at tiktok.com.

Step: Click the button below. A new TikTok tab will open automatically and the extension will begin the removal process.

  • Opens your TikTok profile
  • Goes to the "Liked" tab
  • Opens each liked video
  • Removes the like and moves to the next
  • Repeats until your liked videos list is empty

Note: If you have many liked videos, the process may take a while. TikTok may temporarily block actions if too many happen quickly. If that occurs, wait about 1 hour and try again.

Tip: When the process finishes, refresh your profile to verify the videos were unliked successfully.
